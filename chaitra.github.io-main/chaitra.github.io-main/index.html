About Us

Greetings from CHAITRA TELUGU ASSOCIATION...!

   

We are excited to announce a new association “ CHAITRA TELUGU ASSOCIATION” for the academic year 2021-2022.

'CHAITRA' means Vasantham. The main purpose of this association is to promote the importance of Telugu Sanskriti on the campus and around the world.

The goals of the CHAITRA Association are as below.
1. To experience the rich culture and heritage of Telugu nativity.
2. To provide a platform for students to improve the traditional values and to strengthen the coordination among all in celebrating Telugu festivals.
3. Organizing National and International Telugu Association Days by inviting guest speakers who have splendid knowledge about our Indian culture and Telugu nativity.
4. Organize programs like Rangoli, Games and celebrate all Telugu festivals and Telugu Cultural events in the Campus. 

 

The CHAITRA association aims to be alive and active by organizing events to engage the students in a happy and glorious way with fun-filled moments.
